About NMTC Tamale

The Nurses' and Midwives' Training College (NMTC) Tamale is a premier institution dedicated to training highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals. Established with a vision to address the healthcare needs of Ghana and beyond, NMTC Tamale has a rich history of academic excellence and community service.

Our mission is to provide quality nursing and midwifery education, foster critical thinking, promote ethical practice, and instill a lifelong commitment to professional development. We believe in creating an environment that encourages innovation, research, and holistic growth for all our students.

At NMTC Tamale, we are committed to shaping the future of healthcare by producing competent, confident, and caring nurses and midwives who are ready to make a significant impact in their communities.
