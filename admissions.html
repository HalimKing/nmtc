Admission Requirements

General Requirements:

  • Applicants must be at least 18 years old at the time of application.
  • Possession of a Senior High School Certificate (WASSCE/SSSCE) or its equivalent.
  • Passes in core subjects: English Language, Mathematics, Integrated Science.
  • Passes in three elective subjects relevant to the chosen program.
  • Good conduct and moral character.

Program-Specific Requirements:

  • **Diploma in General Nursing:** Credit passes (A1-C6 for WASSCE, A-D for SSSCE) in core subjects and three elective science subjects (e.g., Biology, Chemistry, Physics).
  • **Diploma in Registered Midwifery:** Credit passes in core subjects and three elective science subjects. Female applicants only.
  • **Diploma in Post NAC/NAP Midwifery:** Must be a certified NAC/NAP with at least two years of post-qualification experience, plus credit passes in core subjects.

For detailed requirements and specific subject combinations, please refer to our official admission brochure.
