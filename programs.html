Diploma in General Nursing

Program Overview

Our three-year Diploma in General Nursing program is designed to prepare competent, caring, and skilled registered nurses. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with extensive clinical practice to ensure graduates are ready for professional practice.

Core Subjects

  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Fundamentals of Nursing
  • Medical-Surgical Nursing
  • Pediatric Nursing
  • Mental Health Nursing
  • Community Health Nursing
  • Pharmacology
  • Nursing Research and Ethics

Diploma in Registered Midwifery

Program Overview

Our comprehensive three-year midwifery program prepares skilled midwives to provide expert care throughout the childbearing process. Students learn to support women and families during pregnancy, labor, birth, and the postnatal period.

Core Subjects

  • Anatomy and Physiology of Reproduction
  • Antenatal Care and Management
  • Normal and Abnormal Labor
  • Postnatal Care
  • Newborn Care and Resuscitation
  • Family Planning and Reproductive Health
  • Maternal and Child Nutrition
  • Midwifery Ethics and Professional Practice

Post NAC/NAP Midwifery

Program Overview

This intensive two-year program is designed for qualified Nursing Assistant Clinical (NAC) and Nursing Assistant Preventive (NAP) professionals who wish to advance their careers in midwifery. The program builds upon existing healthcare knowledge and skills.

Program Structure

  • Advanced Reproductive Health
  • High-Risk Pregnancy Management
  • Emergency Obstetric Care
  • Neonatal Care and Management
  • Family Planning Counseling
  • Leadership in Midwifery Practice
  • Research Methods in Midwifery
  • Quality Improvement in Maternal Care
